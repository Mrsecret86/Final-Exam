Chip
"Phase Technician"
  • Role: Support/Tank
  • Lane Reccomendation: Roaming
  • Price: 32000 or 599
  • Skill Resource: Mana
  • Damage Type: Magic
  • Basic Attack Type: Melee and Ranged
Chip Skills

Passive Skill - Snack Time!

When Chip is not in fighting condition, he will eat potato chips which can
regenerate HP by 150 plus 15% if he is able to finish his bag of potato chips. This
passive has a cooldown of 5 seconds.

With this passive, Chip really benefits, especially in rotation conditions. He will get
additional laughter when he changes lanes and is in good enough condition to
fight on another lane .

Skill 1 - Crash Course

After this skill is activated, the resulting basic attack will then be long range. If the
basic attack hits all enemies around the mark, the mark will explode and give
magic damage and a stun effect for 0.5 seconds.

Chip smashes the hovercraft he is riding in to the ground, producing Magic
Damage and giving a Mark to the enemy hero who is hit. After casting this skill,
Chip also gets a Shield which increases along with the number of enemies
affected by this skill.

Skill 2 - Overtime

Chip crashes forward for 2 seconds, increasing additional Movement Speed by
65% for 2 seconds. After that, it will enter charge mode. In this mode, basic
attacks on the enemy provide magic damage and a knockback effect.

Apart from providing a CC effect towards the enemy, this skill can also be used to
increase speed so that the rotation process to another lane takes place more
quickly.

Ultimate Skill - Shortcuts

Chip releases a Main Portal on enemy heroes, dealing Magic Damage and slowing
them by 30% for 2.5 seconds. Along with that, a connecting portal will appear
near teammates. The main portal and connecting portal will last up to 10 seconds.

Either enemies or teammates can teleport from the connecting portal to the main
portal, or stand at the main portal for a certain period of time to return to their
original location. The main portal can cause damage to nearby enemies after a
delay and provide an airborne effect after a short delay . The main portal has 80%
of the total life of the Chip itself.

Special Skill - Why Walk

Chip also has a special skill called Why Walk. The effect of this skill is that every
time this hero appears in a match, 4 beacons will automatically appear in the
game. With this beacon, he can teleport to other beacon positions . To activate
this beacon, he must stand on the beacon, and have the portal number he wants
to go to.

Portal Beacon

Please note that the portal beacon can only be used by a Chip. Apart from that,
the cooldown of this skill is quite long, namely 40 seconds. Under these
conditions, players must be careful and think carefully about the time and position
needed to use this skill.
