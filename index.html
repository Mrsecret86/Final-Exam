What Is Mobile Legends: Bang Bang ?

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a mobile video game published by in the multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) genre developed
and published by Moonton, a subsidiary of ByteDance. Mobile Legends is a MOBA game designed for mobile phones.
Both teams of five people each fight to reach and destroy the enemy base while defending their own base in order
to control the three paths, known as the "top" or "Explane/Goldlane", "middle" or "Midlane" and "bottom" or "Explane/Goldlane", that connect to each base.
